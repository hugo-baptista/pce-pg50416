Some of My Favourites Video Games

Bioshock

Bioshock is a first-person shooter game published by 2K Games.

Bioshock Trailer Video

Lore

In the 1940s, Andrew Ryan created a utopia for society's elite to flourish outside of government control and "petty morality", a underwater city named Rapture. This philosophy resulted in remarkable advances in the arts and sciences, which included the discovery of "ADAM": a potent gene-altering substance which is created by a species of sea slug on the ocean floor.

Story

The story takes place in 1960, where Jack, the sole survivor of a plane crash in the middle of the Atlantic Ocean, discovers the entrance to the recluse underwater city, at the end of a civil war which left most of it in disrepair. Finding himself trapped in a strange and dangerous dystopia, and with only a mysterious man called Atlas helping him, Jack has no choice but to fight for his survival against Rapture's mutated and monstrous denizens, using all types of weapons and genetic enhancements, as he searches for a way to return to the surface.

Other Games

This game franchise has 2 other games: Bioshock 2, in the 1970s Rapture, and Bioshock Infitite, on a floating steampunk city-state called Columbia in the 1920s.

Resident Evil 2

Resident Evil is a video game franchise created by Capcom. Most of this games consis of survival horror.

Resident Evil 2 Trailer Video

Lore

Umbrella Corporationan is a international pharmaceutical company that secretly develops mutagenic viruses to further their "bio-organic weapons" research, most of which can tranform people into mindless zombies.

Story

29th of September of 1998, Leon S. Kennedy makes his way toward Raccoon City to start his first shift at the police department. He stops at a gas station on the city outskirts, where he is overrun by zombies, caused by an outbreak of the T-Virus, created in the city's secret Umbrella's lab. On his fight to get back to the car, he meets Claire Redfield, who is looking for her missing brother. Now, they both need to find a way out of the city, before the American Government nukes it, to put an end to the virus outbreak.

Other Games

Resident Evil 0, 1, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, Revelations, Revelations 2, Code Verionica and much more!

Kingdom Come Deliverance

Developed and published by Warhorse Studios, Kingdom Come Deliverance is a RPG (Role-Playing Game) that takes place in the 1400s Bohemia (today in Czechia).

Kingdom Come Deliverance Trailer Video

Lore

King Wenceslaus IV of Bohemia is the current king, but his half-brother King Sigismund of Hungary wants to usurp him. For that, he gets Cuman mercenaries, remainings of the Mongol Empire, to raid and cause instability in the kingdom.

Story

Henry, son of the blacksmith of Skalitz, is one of the survivors of the massacre caused by the Cumans in its village, which caused the death of both his parents. In the pursuit of justice for his family, he joins the service of Lord Radzig Kobyla, who leads a resistance movement against Sigismund's invasion.

Other Games

For now, this is the only game of the franchise, but the developers have already confirmed that they're working on the second game, that continues Henry's story where the first one lefts it.
